Raising Miniature Poodles in Mid-Coast Maine

Our pups are purebread and AKC miniature poodles. They are a lovely variety of colors including cream, red, apricot, and chocolate. Poodles are known for their soft, curly, and hypo-allergenic coat. They are affectionate, intelligent and make excellent companions. Because they are so social and love all ages, them make great family dogs.
